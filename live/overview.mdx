## What is $LIVE?
It serves as the backbone of the LiveSuite platform, enabling a sustainable economic model where platform growth directly benefits token holders. As LiveSuite continues to expand its suite of tools for traders and streamers, $LIVE will become increasingly integrated into the platform's core functionality.

## Economic Model

The $LIVE token economy is designed to create a sustainable flywheel:

1. **Platform Growth** → LiveSuite generates revenue from its tools and services
2. **Revenue Distribution** → Portions allocated to buy backs and revenue sharing
3. **Value Accrual** → Token holders benefit from platform success
4. **Increased Adoption** → Growing utility drives demand for $LIVE
5. **Enhanced Platform** → More resources enable better features and services

## Benefits for Holders

- **Revenue Sharing**: Earn from platform success
- **Buy Back Program**: Regular supply reduction supports price appreciation
- **Platform Access**: Unlock premium features and discounts
- **Future Governance**: Participate in platform decisions
- **Exclusive Features**: Early access to new tools and integrations

## Long-Term Vision

As LiveSuite evolves, $LIVE will become increasingly central to the platform experience. The token will serve as both a utility asset for accessing premium features and a value-accruing asset that benefits from the platform's continued growth and success in the pump.fun ecosystem.
