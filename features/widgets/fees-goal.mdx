Track total creator fees collected toward a specific goal amount. Show your earnings progress in real-time.

## Configuration

**Required:**
- **Creator Address** - Wallet address of token creator
- **Goal Amount (SOL)** - Target fees in SOL (supports decimals like `100.5`)

**Optional:**
- **Accent Color** - Customizable accent color

## Widget URL

**Basic:**
```
https://livesuite.app/widgets/fees-goal/YOUR_CREATOR_ADDRESS/50
```

**With Decimal Goal:**
```
https://livesuite.app/widgets/fees-goal/YOUR_CREATOR_ADDRESS/100.5?accentColor=#ff8800
```

- Fee tracking and calculations
- SOL and USD display options
- Progress visualization toward goal
- Goal completion tracking
- Real-time fee updates

## Use Case

Show how much you've earned in creator fees and progress toward your goal. Great for transparency and building community trust.
