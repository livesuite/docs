Get real-time streaming data in your Telegram groups and channels. The Telegram Scanner bot brings LiveSuite data directly to your conversations.

## Features

- Real-time streaming data
- Token information queries
- Custom commands
- Group and channel integration

## Use Cases

- Monitor tokens in your Telegram groups
- Get quick stats without leaving Telegram
- Share data with your community
- Automated alerts and notifications

1. Add the LiveSuite bot to your Telegram group or channel
2. Use commands to query token data
3. Configure alerts and notifications
4. Share streaming stats with your community
