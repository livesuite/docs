Calculate creator fees based on trading volume. Perfect for streamers tracking earnings or traders estimating costs.

## How It Works

Enter your expected trading volume, and we'll calculate:
- Total creator fees (typically 1% of volume)
- Fees in SOL
- Fees in USD
- Breakdown by volume ranges

**Features:**
- Volume-based fee calculations
- Real-time SOL price integration
- USD conversion
- Interactive volume sliders
- Daily, weekly, and monthly projections
- Market cap-based fee adjustments

## Using the Calculator

1. Set your market cap (affects fee percentage)
2. Set your daily trading volume
3. Choose time period (Daily, Weekly, Monthly)
4. See earnings projections in SOL and USD

<Tip>
  Creator fees are typically 1% of all buy and sell volume. The calculator helps you understand what that means in real terms.
</Tip>

## Use Cases

- Streamers estimating earnings potential
- Traders calculating trading costs
- Project owners planning fee structures
- Understanding fee accumulation over time
