LiveSuite provides powerful analytics tools to help you understand token performance, viewer behavior, and market trends. Whether you're analyzing your own streams or scouting opportunities, our visualizations make data easy to understand.

## Analytics Dashboard

Our analytics dashboard is split into three main tabs, each focused on different aspects of token and platform data.

### Overview Tab

Get a high-level view of platform-wide metrics and trends.

**Features:**
- Global statistics with sparkline trends
- Interactive treemaps showing token distribution
- Real-time data tables (optimized for desktop and mobile)
- Live updates automatically
- Pull-to-refresh on mobile

**Global Stats:**
- Total Streamers, Live Streamers, Total Viewers, Avg Session Duration
- Animated number displays with smooth transitions
- Sparkline charts showing 24-hour trends
- Live status indicators

**Treemaps:** 
- Dual treemap display: Top 10 Tokens by Viewers AND Top 10 Tokens by Time side-by-side
- Interactive token cards with click-through navigation
- Size-based visual hierarchy (larger = higher value)
- Token images, names, symbols, and metrics
- Hover tooltips for quick info

### Performance Tab

Financial analytics and market insights.

**Market Cap Distribution:**
- See how tokens are distributed across market cap ranges
- Donut charts showing percentage breakdowns
- Identify trends in token valuations

**Token Age Analysis:**
- Track how token age correlates with performance
- Understand lifecycle patterns
- Spot opportunities in new vs. established tokens

**Market Trends:**
- Average market cap over time
- Growth patterns and anomalies
- Peak performance timelines
- Market cap flex images showing distribution

### Activity Patterns Tab

Activity patterns and consistency metrics.

**Activity Heatmaps:**
- **Daily heatmaps** - Hour-by-hour patterns with day of week visualization
- **Monthly heatmaps** - Day-by-day activity over months for long-term trend identification
- **Consistency heatmaps** - Streaming consistency visualization with scoring

Heatmaps use color intensity to show activity levels—darker means more activity. Interactive hover details show exact values and timestamps.

**Stream Tracker Charts:**
- Multi-line chart with 4 data series: Messages (green), Joins (blue), Leaves (orange), Unique Users (magenta)
- 24-hour configurable data window
- Real-time updates every 30 seconds
- Interactive legend for toggling series
- Summary metrics: Total Messages, Total Joins/Leaves, Peak Unique Users

## Visualization Types

### Treemap Charts

Interactive squares sized by market cap or viewers. Perfect for spotting the biggest tokens at a glance. Click any square to dive deeper.

**Use Cases:**
- Quick overview of top performers
- Identifying hidden gems
- Understanding market concentration

### Heatmaps

Color-coded grids showing activity patterns over time. Great for understanding when tokens are most active.

**Use Cases:**
- Finding optimal streaming times
- Identifying consistency patterns
- Spotting unusual activity

### Sparkline Charts

Mini trend indicators embedded in stat bars. See at a glance whether metrics are trending up or down.

**Use Cases:**
- Quick trend assessment
- Real-time direction indicators
- Historical context

### Multi-Line Charts

Compare multiple metrics over time on the same chart. Perfect for understanding relationships between different data points.

**Use Cases:**
- Viewers vs. market cap correlation
- Price vs. engagement trends
- Multiple token comparison

### Donut Charts

Circular charts showing distribution percentages. Great for understanding market segmentation.

**Use Cases:**
- Market cap distribution buckets
- Viewer distribution analysis
- Token category breakdowns

## Reading the Data

**Viewer Trends:** Upward trends in viewer counts typically indicate growing interest. Spikes can mean viral moments or coordinated activity.

**Market Cap Correlation:** Tokens with high market caps and high viewers often have stronger fundamentals. Use correlation analysis to find undervalued opportunities.

**Consistency Patterns:** Regular streaming schedules build stronger communities. Look for consistent patterns in successful tokens.

**Heatmap Insights:** Darker periods indicate higher activity. Patterns can reveal optimal streaming times or detect unusual bot activity.
